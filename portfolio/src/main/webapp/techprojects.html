Roombaby

The roomba has always been a really interesting invention to me, which is why when my security course asked us to do a project on anything we wanted as long as it related to security, of course I decided to make a beeline for a secondhand roomba to expose its vulnerabilities - however, this didn't work out, considering I had no experience in anything of the sort - but I did write an application to send commands to it, the most interesting (in my opinion) being the ability to send it hex codes to play particular musical notes, essentially allowing you to get your roomba to 'sing'.
